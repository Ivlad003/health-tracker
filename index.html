Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a WHOOP device?
A WHOOP device and active membership are required for activity, recovery, and sleep tracking features. You can still use calorie tracking and meal logging without WHOOP.
Is my health data secure?
Yes. We use encrypted database storage, secure OAuth 2.0 for API connections, and never share your data with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
How does calorie tracking work?
Send a message to our Telegram bot with what you ate — by text or voice. We search the FatSecret database (500K+ foods) and log the nutritional data automatically.
Is it really free?
Yes, Health Tracker is free to use. You need your own WHOOP membership for activity data, but the bot and calorie tracking are completely free.
